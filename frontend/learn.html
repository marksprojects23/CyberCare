Welcome to CyberCare

CyberCare is a free web browser extension designed to protect you while you browse the internet.

Why Use CyberCare?

CyberCare helps keep you safe from scams, fake websites, and other online dangers.

How It Works

CyberCare watches over your internet browsing and warns you if it detects anything suspicious.
